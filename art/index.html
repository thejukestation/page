ART

certain images, colors or compositions can evoke EMOTIONS that are hard to explain — inspiring, comforting, or sometimes unsettling. i'm fascinated by how VISUAL ART can do that without needing a single word. i've also made some of my own work over the years, mostly in a spontaneous way rather than with a lot of patience or planning — but it's something i want to explore more. platforms like PINTEREST helped expand that fascination and shape my visual taste.
