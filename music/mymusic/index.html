MY MUSIC

2020 — THE BEGINNING

in 2020 my music teacher taught our class to use a simple DAW. it was called SOUNDTRAP, a free software by SPOTIFY themselves. it was very easy to use and i immediately started making my first beats.

2021 — GOING DEEPER

after a couple months i already made a ton of tracks and they got better and better. in 2021 i recorded with an audio interface for the first time. it blew my mind and i finally could play REAL instruments on my songs.

2022 — FIRST ALBUM

in 2022 i made my first album — not on spotify, but only for friends and family. it was called MELTING. after that, i was hungry for more and so i started the next one.

2023 — GOING ONLINE

one year later i started posting online and started my career on spotify and all the other platforms. it didn't perform very well, so i took it down. the music is still online on youtube, and i got some links below to old videos.
