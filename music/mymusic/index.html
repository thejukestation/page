MY MUSIC

2020 — THE BEGINNING

in 2020 my music teacher taught our class to use a simple DAW. it was called SOUNDTRAP, a free website that was owned by SPOTIFY at the time. it was very easy to use and i immediately started making my first beats.

2021 — GOING DEEPER

after a couple months i already made a ton of tracks and they got better and better. in 2021 i recorded with an AUDIO INTERFACE for the first time. it blew my mind and i finally could play REAL instruments on my songs.

2022 — FIRST ALBUM

in 2022 i made my first album — not on spotify, but only for friends and family. it was called MELTING. after that, i was hungry for more and so i started the next one.

2023 — GOING ONLINE

one year later i started posting online and started my career on SPOTIFY and all the other platforms. it didn't perform very well, so i took it down. some of the music is still online on YOUTUBE though.

2024 — A NEW WORLD

in 2024 i bought my first own microphone. a whole new world opened. i could record my music through air! sounds quite normal but it changed me completely. i did some demos and even recorded drums, only with one mic, the AUDIO TECHNICA AT2020. the songs are still shelved but i'd like to record it one day.

2025 — VIENNA & LOGIC PRO

then in 2025 i started my studies in VIENNA as an AUDIO ENGINEER and i bought a MACBOOK and LOGIC PRO. and this... completely changed everything. my tracks finally sounded like i've always imagined them. of course i got better over the years but a SOFTWARE with good PLUGINS and VSTs did the rest. and so only after one month of work i did a whole album, THE EAST END. it was just meant to be a simple but groovy album to listen to while doing something else.

2026 — STORIES IN MOTION

later in 2025 i REALLY wanted to reinvent myself, deleted my past work as PRONOY and started the album STORIES IN MOTION. i came up with the name JUKE, which is just my first and last name combined lol. and now in 2026 i am really pushing my limits with this album and i hope that you will give my album a try!
